Detailed Description

Cut out of the parents structure due to a control surface.

Optional. Definition of the parents structure cut out due to a control surface. The cut out is split into three parts: cut out of the upper and lower skin and the definition of an profile conecting the cut out of the upper and lower skin. An example for wing cut outs can be found in the picture below:

See also
wingCutOut In the default configuration the cut out is as wide as the control surface. If additional spacing is necessary inner and outer border may be set.
